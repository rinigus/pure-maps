FAQ

Frequently asked questions:

Can i use pure-maps offline?

Yes, when running Osmscout-server Pure Maps navigates completely offline. See Offline-Navigation

Can I import .gpx tracks?

Yes, but its limited to the navigation module. See GPX-Import

Does Pure Maps display height profiles?

No.

Why can’t I route long trips for walking or cycling in offline mode?

Increase limits in Osmscout-server-gui in Settings > Backend settings > Valhalla > Limits.
