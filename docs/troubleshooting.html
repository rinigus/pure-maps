Troubleshooting

GPS

Pure Maps does not get any location in linux.

Make sure modem/gps is enabled, gnss-share and geoclue are running. If you use ModemManager 1.23.3 or lower, there must be a sim card present. postmarketOS users read: https://wiki.postmarketos.org/wiki/User:Magdesign#GPS

Map

Pure-maps does not show city names in the default offline mode.

Edit: /usr/share/osmscout-server/styles/mapboxgl/styles/osmbright.json change under id: place-city
